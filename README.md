# Chrysanthemum-CRISPR-Tools
About CCPPB

The Chrysanthemum CRISPR Plasmid Primer Builder (CCPPB) is a specialized tool tailored for designing protospacer primers for CRISPR genome editing vectors.

Developed by the Chrysanthemum Genetics and Breeding Research Team at Nanjing Agricultural University.

🚀 Future Updates: We are committed to continuous improvement. Additional design modules for new plasmid architectures and CRISPR systems will be integrated in future updates to meet evolving research needs.
